Audience

Authors Bio's, Index

The Yellow House is for people who have an interest in escape rooms and an interest in exploring abandoned places, especially in video games, without the element of horror. Beyond the adventure, The Yellow House has touching secrets that are unexpected, adding an emotional layer to the exploration. Enter into a narrative that combines the thrill of problem solving and uncovering secrets with a touch of emotion. If you appreciate interactive storytelling and the joy of discovery, The Yellow House is your ideal adventure.
