The Yellow House

Authors Bio's, About

You cautiously try to open the rusted gate surrounding the house. The weeds and vines make it a bit difficult but with a bit of a running start you finally get the gate open. Having to move some leaves and branches from hitting your face you make it to the backyard. To your left, a small deck with a table, chairs and a door that leads back to the house. To your right sits a run down shed that is up against the brick wall and what you can infer is a overgrown garden from the wooden rectangles on the floor.

Check out the overgrown garden. Try to open the garden shed.
