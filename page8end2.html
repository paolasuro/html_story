THE END

Authors Bio's, About

You open your eyes still dizzy as you slowly grab your phone out of your pocket. It's as if you didn't know how to work a phone. Your brain feels like scrambled eggs but you push through and find the keypad and dial 911. You tell the operator as much as you can without throwing up or passing out. The ambulance is on its way but with your head spinning uncontrollably it feels like a lifetime. When they finally arrive you barely have the strength to scream “I'm here! Help me!” You hear the sound of footsteps as you lose the strength to keep your eyes closed.

When you wake up, you're surrounded but the faces of your family and friends. They tell you how they got a call from the hospital about you. Appearently, the blueberries were not blueberries at all but poisonous berries that if you eat too much of could be deadly. But you were lucky they found you in time or who knows what could have happened.

Start over.
