The Yellow House

Authors Bio's, About

You dust yourself off and start heading over towards the shed, your palms brushing against each other to get rid of any lingering dirt. As you approach the door, you struggle to open the door at first as it has rusted over the years. But after failing a few times you give the door one big tug and you finally get the door open. You pull out your phone to use as a flashlight and start searching. You find a few tools but not what you were looking for, in the back of the shed, leaning up against the wall was a shovel.

Explore the overgrown garden. Go into the house.
