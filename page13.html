The Yellow House

Authors Bio's, About

You immediately start digging with your hands. Your hands, arms, and legs quickly get covered in the dirt but you're too eager to know what's inside the chest to care about how dirty you are. You start to get antsy and tired as your arms and legs start to hurt from the endless digging. However, you push through the pain, and you finally get to the chest, you drag it out of the hole with the leftover strength you have. After taking a second to breath, you step towards the front of the chest only to see a lock on the chest.

Give up and go home. Try to break the lock with the shovel.
