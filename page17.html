The Yellow House

Authors Bio's, About

You open the pantry door, you take out your phone to use as a flashlight. You step inside and find shelves filled with a bunch of jars containing what is most likely expired food. You continue to look around and find some boxes on the floor. You place your phone on one of the shelves above you. You move the boxes out of the way to see what's inside but when you grab your phone to look at the boxes you see a small door. After trying to open it you see that it has a key hole. You get up and start searching throughout the kitchen, checking every cabinet and drawer for a key but you find nothing.

Give up and go home. Try the other side of the house. Go to the back yard.
