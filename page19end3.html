Authors Bio's, About

Quickly but carefully you go back downstairs and go to the pantry, you dig for the key you put it in your pocket and see if it's the right key and it works the lock clicks and you open the door to find a bag. Opening the bag ever so carefully, inside you find a red dress, a long black-haired wig, a red lipstick, and more pictures. You look at the picture and see a woman witha red lip and long black hair wearing the dress in the bag posing around the house you're in right now. But you take a closer look and realize that lady looks familiar. You quickly grab the pictures you find in the chest and begin to look through them once more. You stop at a picture taken of two men together at the beach. You hold the pictures against each other. One of the men had the same striking blue eyes as the woman in the red dress. You flip over the picture of the woman in the dress and read "The only time I've ever felt like myself" A tear rolls down your eye as you place everything back in the bag. You close the small door and head back outside to put the journal, old pictures, and key back in the chest. You cover up the chest and put everything back like it was and walk home as the sunsets.

Start over.
