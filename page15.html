The Yellow House

Slowly, you step on the stairs that lead to the front door. Hoping that the old wood won't give way and break on you. You make it to the front door. You look behind you once again to make sure if anyone might happen to see you but there's nothing there. Your hand shakes as it slowly reaches out toward the door handle. You twist the handle and to your surprise, it opens. The door makes a loud creaking sound as you open it. You step into the house and quickly shut the door behind you. You take a second to look around you.

Go left to the stairs. Go right to the kitchen.
