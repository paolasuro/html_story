The Yellow House

Authors Bio's, About

You start by exploring the left side of the overgrown garden. But you find nothing of interest. All that’s left is to turn around and explore the right side of the overgrown garden. When you get there and start looking around your shoe catches on to something and you trip. You scrape your knee a bit, and a few drops of blood slide down your leg, you might get a bruise in a few days but nothing you can’t handle. After ensuring your leg is fine you look at what you tripped on. It seems to be a piece of wood. You try to grab it but it’s too heavy. You move some of the dirt around and realize it’s a chest.

Dig it up with your hands. Try to find something to help you dig it up in the shed.
