The Yellow House

Authors Bio's, About

You pick one blueberry and pop it into your mouth. They're sweet and refreshing and your hungry stomach thanks you for the food. You go to grab some more but your stomach starts to hurt a bit. You look at the blueberries once more, you decide it's best to not have anymore and you let them fall to the ground. Even though your stomach still hurts it's nothing you can't push through. Now you're left with a decision to make.

Dig it up with your hands. Try to find something to help you dig it up in the shed.
